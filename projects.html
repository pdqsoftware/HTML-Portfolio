Learn French Vocubulary

A random French word, from a list, is displayed on a white flipcard for three seconds before the translated English word is displayed on a green flipcard.

If the learner knows the word being shown he/she can click on the green tick button to remove the word from the list. Selecting the red cross button will result in the next word being shown.

The words are displayed over and over until each one has been memorized and thus removed from the list.
